About us

Fresh is Best

P.S. Restaurant opened in 1984 by Thai Chef Preecha Songprastit. His vision was a comfortable atmosphere with amazing food. Preecha created the basis for our French, Thai fusion menu. In 1990 Rick and Sylvana purchased P.S. and through the past 25 years they have created an atmosphere of fine dining in casual elegance. After expanding in 1995 with the addition of our beautiful lounge, P.S. continued to grow adding our catering venue in 2006. Our wine list has been enhanced from Preecha's ten wines to over 400 selections. In 1994 P.S. was the first restaurant in the area to earn the prestigious Wine Spectator award of excellence and continued to receive the award year after year.

Our mission is simple: "Fresh is Best." We support local farmers and everything is personally prepared by our owner/ chef Rick Dodd. Our atmosphere is casual yet elegant and from the moment you walk through our door you are a guest in our restaurant!!!

Our Philosophy

Our basic philosophy at P.S. is from the moment you walk in our door you are a guest in our restaurant. From our first greeting to your last goodbye, our casual, yet attentive servers will do everything possible to make you feel at home in our restaurant. As for our food, we have but one phrase, “Fresh is best,” whether it be seafood, meat, poultry or vegetables, only the finest quality and freshest ingredients available come out of our kitchen. In keeping with our “Fresh is Best” policy all items are personally prepared to order by our owner/chef. Some items may require additional preparation time. We hope this will not be an inconvenience. If for any reason whatsoever, your dining experience does not meet your expectations, we ask you to let a member of our staff know immediately. Our primary goal is to have you raving about our service and cuisine and we will do everything in our power to achieve that goal. We appreciate and thank you for your patronage.

Your Hosts,
